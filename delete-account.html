Account & Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your Green Gift account and remove all associated personal data, you can request it at any time using the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, send an email to:

Email: princechaturvedi37@gmail.com

Include the following details in your message:

  • Your registered mobile number
  • Your Name
  • Mention "Account Deletion Request"

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon verification, we will permanently delete:

  • User profile information (name & phone number)
  • Order history associated with your account
  • Addresses & delivery details

Data Retention

Some transaction details may be retained only where legally required (ex. tax/GST invoices). All profile information will be fully removed within 7 days of request.

Confirmation

You will receive an email confirmation once your data is deleted.
