Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how GreenGift (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application GreenGift (the “App”) and related services.

By installing or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide to us

  • Account Information: When you create an account or place an order, we may collect information such as your name, mobile number, email address (if provided), and password or OTP-based login details.
  • Delivery Details: We collect your delivery address, PIN code, and contact details so that we can deliver plants and gifts to the correct location.
  • Order Information: Details about the products you browse, add to cart, purchase, and gift (e.g., plant type, quantity, price, delivery date, recipient name/phone if provided).
  • Support & Communication: If you contact us for support or feedback, we may collect the information you choose to share, including screenshots or order details.

1.2 Payment and transaction information

We use third-party payment providers (such as UPI, wallet or payment gateway providers) to process payments. We do not store your full payment card details on our servers. However, we may receive limited information such as payment status, transaction ID, payment method (UPI, card, wallet), and the amount paid so that we can confirm and track your orders.

1.3 Automatically collected information

  • Device & App Information: We may collect information about your device, such as device model, operating system version, App version, and unique device identifiers for the purpose of improving app performance and troubleshooting.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with the App, such as the screens you view, features you use, and time spent in the App. This helps us understand and improve the user experience.

1.4 Location information

In some cases, we may use or request access to your approximate location (for example, based on PIN code or device location) to show nearby delivery availability or estimate delivery charges. We do not collect your precise GPS location unless explicitly required and permitted by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and deliver your orders and gifts.
  • To send you order confirmations, delivery updates, and important notifications.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your queries.
  • To improve, personalize, and develop our App and services.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse of the App.
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid requests from authorities.

3. Legal Basis (Where Applicable)

Depending on your location, we may process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your consent (for example, when you create an account or agree to receive notifications).
  • Performance of a contract (to process and deliver your orders).
  • Legitimate interests (such as improving our services and preventing fraud).
  • Compliance with a legal obligation.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Such as payment gateways, cloud hosting providers (for example, Supabase as our backend database and authentication provider), SMS/OTP providers, and delivery partners who help us provide the service.
  • Business Partners: Only when necessary to complete your order (e.g., local nurseries or delivery service partners).
  • Legal & Safety: If required by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, the public, or our business.

These third parties are only allowed to use your information to provide services to us and are required to protect your information.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your information on secure servers and use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We keep your personal data for as long as necessary to provide the services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

For example, we may retain basic account and transaction information for tax, accounting, or legal compliance.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or update of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on your consent for processing.

To exercise these rights or to request deletion of your account, you can contact us using the details provided in the “Contact Us” section below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies separately.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. We recommend that you review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
